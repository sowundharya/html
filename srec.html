About Us

Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College (SREC), Coimbatore established in the year 1994 by SNR Sons Charitable Trust is one of the 17 institutions managed by the trust. SREC is an autonomous institution offering 10 Undergraduate programmes in the disciplines of Aeronautical, Biomedical, Civil, Mechanical, Electronics and Communication, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics and Instrumentation, Information Technology,Computer Science and Engineering and Robotics and Automation. The institution offers Seven Post Graduate programmes in Engineering and Technology with specializations of Manufacuring Engineering, Power Electronics and Drives, VLSI Design, Computer Science and Engineering , Embedded System Technologies, Control and Instrumentation Engineering and Nanoscience and Technology, in addition to MBA. Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College is accredited by NAAC with ‘A’ grade and Seven of the B.E/ B.Tech programmes have been accredited and re - accredited by National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi, since 2003. The institution currently has 4400 plus students passionately mentored by 279 faculty members of whom 97 are holding Ph.D, with 120 pursuing Ph.D A very good research culture exists in the campus with an average annual funding of Rs. 3.4 crore from various funding agencies - AICTE, DST, CSIR, DRDO etc., The institution is also funded through DST- FIST and DST- INSPIRE and DST- NST MIS.

Placement

SREC boasts an excellent environment for academic development and professional advancement. The goal-driven career development and placement cell incessantly trains the students ensuring placement in companies of repute. It also helps the students who are interested in pursuance of higher education. Able guidance is provided to the ones who have entrepreneurially inclined. The cell nurtures industry-institution interaction through in-plant training, internship and projects of industrial relevance.
